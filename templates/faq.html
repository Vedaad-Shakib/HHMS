FAQ

I don't feel safe inputting my credentials into this unofficial website. What happens to my login information after I press the login button?

Your login information is never stored in our system. It is merely passed on to PCR, which then returns the information we need.

Why is this site so slow?

HHMS severely depends on PCR to attain the information it requires. As you may have noticed, PCR is extremely slow, and accessing information in PCR from an external source takes even more time.

I found a bug. Will you fix it?

Send it to 17vedaads@students.harker.org or 17andrewt@students.harker.org, and we'll look at it.
